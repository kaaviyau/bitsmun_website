OUR COMMITTEES

We are excited to announce UN Security Council as a committee for the 14th Edition of BITSMUN Goa. The United Nations Security Council (UNSC) is one of the six main organs of the United Nations and is responsible for maintaining international peace and security.

We are excited to announce UN Women as a committee for the 14th Edition of BITSMUN Goa. UN Wome is dedicated to advancing gender equality and women's rights globally. They work to eliminate discrimination, promote women empowerment.

We are excited to announce UN General Assembly as a committee for the 14th Edition of BITSMUN Goa. DISEC is the UN General Assembly's First Committee, focused on disarmament, global security, and finding solutions to threats to peace.

We are excited to announce UN Security Council as a committee for the 14th Edition of BITSMUN Goa. The United Nations Security Council (UNSC) is one of the six main organs of the United Nations and is responsible for maintaining international peace and security.
