COUNTRY MATRIX

The country matrix for BITSMUN includes a diverse range of countrys, each with a unique focus and purpose. Some of the countrys focus on international relations and diplomacy, while others are centered on topics such as climate change and cybersecurity. Each country is chaired by experienced individuals who bring a wealth of knowledge and expertise to the table, helping to ensure that the discussions and debates are productive and informative. Overall, the country matrix for BITSMUN is designed to encourage collaboration and facilitate the exchange of ideas and perspectives, fostering an environment of open communication and intellectual growth.
