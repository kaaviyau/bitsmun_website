China's Evergrande collapse

In recent months one of China’s biggest real estate developers, Evergrande, had to beef up private security outside its Headquarters in Shenzhen, China. This unusual move by Evergrande was made in response to increased protests by small investors, unpaid suppliers and people distressed with incomplete houses that they had already purchased. A rare sight to see in a country like China where protests of any kind are met by brutal repression by the top-down tyrannical Communist government.
