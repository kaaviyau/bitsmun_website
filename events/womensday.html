Event: International Women's Day Celebrations


Date: 8-9 March 2021

On the occassion of International Woman's Day, a webinar with a well curated set of panelists was organized to celebrate 'Stritva' or Womanhood. Interactive sessions with experts about stories of women from diverse backgrounds and cultural programs were held to to celebrate the success stories of woman and girls during the COVID-19 pandemic.
